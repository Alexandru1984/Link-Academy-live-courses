Our History


The very first Starbuzzz opened its doors in 1996. The Victoriei 60 was born from Marian's love for coffee and Daniela's love for the 'big city ritual' he encountered in New York and London.

In the following years, Starbuzzz conquered Bucharest with locations such as the P-ta. Unirii, Dorobanti, and Universitate square. Starbuzzz seduced a new generation of coffee fans with the versatility of coffee in a relaxed environment.

There are now 35 branches in Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Timisoara, Iasi, Oradea, Constanta and Tg. Mures and a roasting / tasting room in Buchares. Each with a unique place in the neighborhood. There are regular performances and there is room on the wall for local designers and artists.

With Starbuzzz, Marian & Daniela ware able to substantiate thirs personal belief that coffee beans are lumped together far too much, and are treated as an anonymous commodity on the futures market.

With purpose-built espresso machines and extensive barista training in the coffee warehouse in Bucharest, brewing coffee at Starbuzzz is once again a craft. The CC Barista Cup has been held annually since 1998, where all baristas compete for the main prizes while grinding, tamping, foaming and latte art. At a national level, baristas can regularly be found in the finals of the Barista Championship, NK Latte art and other competitions.

Continuously looking for the tastiest coffees in the countries of origin, further developing knowledge in the field of roasting and final brewing, is what Starbuzzz does to help as many people as possible get better coffee. Starbuzzz is an independently operating company under the umbrella of Jacobs Douwe Egberts. The passion for and knowledge about coffee is shared back and forth.
